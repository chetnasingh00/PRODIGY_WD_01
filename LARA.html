Our Story

Founded in 2015, LARA was born from a desire to create watches that blend minimalist design with exceptional craftsmanship. Each timepiece is designed to be both functional and beautiful, becoming a cherished part of your daily life.

Our collections range from soft pastel designs that bring a touch of whimsy to your wardrobe, to our Old Money aesthetic pieces that evoke timeless sophistication. Every watch is made with ethically sourced materials and designed to last for generations.

At LARA, we believe a watch should be more than just a timekeeper - it should be a reflection of personal style and a companion for life's most meaningful moments.
